Will the online MCA be recognized while applying for jobs?

Yes, VIT's online MCA degree is UGC-entitled and AICTE-approved and accepted by governments, corporate organizations, and higher education institutions.

I am a fresher. Am I eligible to apply?

There is no minimum work-experience criteria. If you qualify the eligibility criteria, you are eligible.

What is the duration of the MCA programme?

The MCA programme is 24 months long, delivered in a mixed mode format with both recorded and live sessions.

How many specializations are available?

The programme offers 10+ diverse electives from various domains including AI, ML, Cybersecurity, Blockchain, and more.

Is this programme equivalent to an on-campus MCA?

Yes, the online MCA degree is comparable to a postgraduate programme obtained via traditional classroom training and holds the same value.

What are the key skills I will gain from this MCA?

The programme is designed to equip you with essential skills like Software Development, Data Structures, AI/ML, Cybersecurity, and more.

Are there any networking opportunities?

Academics

Learning & Evaluation

How will the participants be evaluated?

Participants will be evaluated through a combination of internal assessment and an end-term examination, with a 30% weightage given to the internal assessment and a 70% weightage assigned to the Final Assessment Test (FAT) or End term exam.

What are the components of Internal Assessment?

Internal Assessment has 3 components: Weekly Quiz, Continuous Assessment Test, and Digital Assignment.

What is a proctored examination?

Final Assessment Test (End term examination) will be conducted in centres in offline mode. Currently, VIT has 20 centres across India. VIT centre list will be expanded.

How are live sessions conducted?

Live sessions are conducted online by VIT faculty, typically 1 hour per week per subject, along with recorded sessions for flexible learning.

What is the curriculum structure?

The programme is divided into 4 semesters over 2 years, with core subjects in the first year and specialization electives in the second year.

Are there any practical components in the curriculum?

Yes, the programme includes hands-on experience with 20+ tools and programming languages, and a project component in the final semester.

What is the minimum passing criteria?

Each participant must get an overall score of at least 50% in each course/subject to successfully complete the programme.

Can I access study materials after course completion?

Yes, students typically have access to course materials for a specified period after programme completion.

Are there any projects in the curriculum?

Yes, the final semester includes a project component as part of the curriculum.

Who are the faculty members?

The programme is taught by VIT's experienced faculty, including Professor Dr. Pounambal M as the Programme Coordinator.

What are the documents required for admission?

For Indian Regular Category: Passport-sized photo, Digital signature, Proof of age (10th Marksheet), 12th Marksheet, ID Card(Aadhar Card), UG mark statements/UG consolidated mark statements, UG provisional/UG degree, Experience certificate (if any), ABC ID and UGC DEB-ID, Community certificate (for reserved category students only).

For NRI/OCI/Foreign Category: Passport-sized photo, Digital signature, 12th Marksheet, Proof of age, Visa/NRI status of parent/candidate: for NRI candidates only, Proof of nationality (passport/birth certificate/national ID), UG mark statements/UG consolidated mark statements, UG provisional/UG degree, Valid score card (if any), Experience certificate (if any).

How can the application form be paid for?

Payment can be made through methods such as Net banking, Credit and Debit cards, and UPI.

What is the eligibility criteria?

Graduate with minimum 50% marks; mathematics subject in 12th standard(10+2). There is no minimum work experience required.

What is the selection process?

Selection is based on eligibility criteria and document verification. There is no entrance exam for this programme.
